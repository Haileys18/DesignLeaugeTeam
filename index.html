Our Inspiration

Rhythym Recycle is a game inspired by the environment around us, and the importance of recyclying and reusing your "trash". Our game tackles 2 different problems: Harsh labor forces, and textile waste that gets thrown into landfills and dumps all around the world. We plan to delve into this issue by creating an environment where the user learns to reusue wasted clothing and fight for the rights of people wgo are forced into hard working conditions in a fun and interactive manner.

How Our Game Works

Rhythm Recycle is a game that relys on 3 mini-games in which revolve around fashion and music. The player needs to win atleast 2 of the mini-games in order to complete the main objective: free the labor workers who are working underneath an evil boss. Once they complete all 3 mini-games and win atleast 2 of them, the boss is defeated and the workers get free'd. However, if they fail to do this task then the workers are trapped inside the game and they lose overall. This way, the player has a fun and interactive experience where they learn to help the people and environment around them.
